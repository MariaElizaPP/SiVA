How to use the SiVA system

To insert a water intake point, fill in the “Intake name” field with the commonly known name of that water intake, along with the municipality, state, the type of supply system (e.g., Collective Alternative Solution (SAC)), and the intake type (e.g., Underground).

To edit a water intake point, select the row you want to modify and fill in the “Intake name” field with the known name of that intake.

To delete a water intake point, select the desired row and press the “Delete” button..
